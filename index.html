Marine Litter Watch: Litter on the beach

The problem of ocean garbage is becoming more and more serious. Some people have made a lot of contributions to people's environment quietly.

They organize the cleaning of beach rubbish without expecting anything in return and deserve everyone's respect.

Community Activity

This graph shows the activity of each community by number of events carried out each year.

We can see from this graph that in 2020, the total number of community activities is the lowest of all years.

We can speculate that this is the cause of the COVID-19, people have reduced their beach cleaning activities.
